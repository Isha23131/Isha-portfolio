In 1921, Gopalganj was elevated to township status which was inhabited by only 3,478 persons. 1925 saw the initiation of Civil Court. In 1936, Muksudpur was split to form Kashiani police station. Gopalganj Mohakuma or sub-division emerged as Gopalganj district on the 1st day of February in 1984.
